Invoice process

From incoming invoice to posting without a media break.

Incoming invoices arrive by email, PDF, XML, XRechnung or upstream systems. Digital Invoice Receipt brings these channels together in one structured process.

ExeQwork connects document processes, SAP DMS, archiving, approval workflows and SAP core processes. It remains clear who reviewed an invoice, which data was transferred and when the invoice can move on.
