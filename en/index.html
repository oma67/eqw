AI product portfolio

AI products for document-centred SAP processes

The core portfolio turns unstructured documents into usable SAP data: BOQ analysis, document classification, sender determination, OCR and AI-supported checks for invoice and document workflows.

Classic SAP development, SAP DMS and migration expertise remain important, but their main role is to make AI results robust inside existing SAP processes.

New: ConcreteCockpit

Bill of quantities documents become SAP quotations

ConcreteCockpit automates quotation preparation for ready-mix concrete producers. It analyses large tender documents, recognises concrete items, derives quantities and prepares quote drafts in SAP ECC or SAP S/4HANA.
