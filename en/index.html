What ExeQwork builds

Software and consulting for document-centred SAP processes

The solutions support teams where documents and SAP processes meet: invoice receipt, SAP DMS, archiving, data and document migration, document classification, search, conversion, signatures and industry-specific quotation processes.

Consulting and development stay closely connected. This creates add-ons that are understandable from a business perspective and fit technically into SAP ECC, SAP S/4HANA, SAP DMS, S/4HANA migrations and classic modules.

New: ConcreteCockpit

Bill of quantities documents become SAP quotations

ConcreteCockpit automates quotation preparation for ready-mix concrete producers. It analyses large tender documents, recognises concrete items, derives quantities and prepares quote drafts in SAP ECC or SAP S/4HANA.
